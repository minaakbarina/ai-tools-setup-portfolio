Post link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/lily-ray-44755615_seo-aioverviews-activity-7353431763068551171-_J_e

### Summary

This post discusses a problem with Google AI Overviews: the AI summary may describe what someone said, but cite the wrong or incomplete sources. Lily Ray highlights how AI-generated answers can summarize information in a way that seems accurate while failing to provide the correct evidence or source attribution.

### Key Insight

The key insight is that AI-powered SEO content production must prioritize source clarity, factual accuracy, and citation reliability. Content should be easy for AI systems and users to verify, with clear authorship, strong references, and well-structured explanations. In the AI search era, being cited correctly is as important as being visible.
