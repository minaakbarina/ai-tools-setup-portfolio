### Summary

This post discusses how marketers can use AI for content strategy beyond simply generating written content. Andy Crestodina explains that content marketing includes many activities, such as auditing calls to action, researching topics, editing content, finding partners or publishing opportunities, and analyzing performance. The post emphasizes that AI can support multiple stages of the content workflow, not just drafting articles.

## LinkedIn Post 2

Date collected: July 7, 2026  
Post link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/andycrestodina_trend-for-2025-whose-job-will-it-be-to-make-activity-7269372309767892992-yhZ2

### Summary

This post discusses the future role of SEO in helping brands get mentioned when users ask AI systems for recommendations. Andy Crestodina frames AI discovery as a new area where SEO professionals may need to influence how brands appear in AI-generated answers and recommendations.

### Key Insight

The key insight is that SEO is expanding from search engine rankings to AI-generated brand visibility. For AI-powered SEO content production, companies need content that helps AI systems understand what the brand does, who it helps, what problems it solves, and why it should be recommended. This requires clear positioning, authoritative content, third-party mentions, and strong topical relevance.
