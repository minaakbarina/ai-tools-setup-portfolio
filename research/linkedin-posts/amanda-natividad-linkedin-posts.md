## LinkedIn Post 2

Date collected: July 7, 2026  
Post link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/amandanat_best-practices-are-the-beige-of-marketing-activity-7432114156843212800-IRVM

### Summary

This post explains that marketing to the average audience often leads to generic messaging. Amanda Natividad emphasizes the importance of finding the audience's digital water cooler and capturing the exact vocabulary that people use. She argues that generic content does not only bore human buyers, but can also be filtered out by AI agents because it lacks a specific voice and clear differentiation.

### Key Insight

The key insight is that AI-powered SEO content should not sound generic. Content teams need to understand the exact language, pain points, objections, and context used by their audience. For B2B SaaS content production, this means using audience research to create specific, differentiated, and useful content that can stand out to both human readers and AI-driven systems.
