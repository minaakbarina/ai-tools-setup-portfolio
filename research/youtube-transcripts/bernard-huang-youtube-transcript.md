## Transcript / Notes

This webinar explains how AI-driven search changes the way users discover information. Instead of only ranking on traditional search results pages, brands now need to be cited, mentioned, and retrieved by AI assistants such as ChatGPT and Google AI Mode.

Bernard Huang discusses how AEO, GEO, and AI SEO are becoming important because search visibility is shifting from keyword rankings to answer visibility. This means content teams need to understand how AI systems retrieve information, summarize answers, and choose which sources to mention.

For AI-powered SEO content production, this material is useful because it shows that content should be structured around clear answers, strong expertise, topical authority, and source credibility.

## Key Insights

1. SEO is shifting from ranking on search results pages to being cited and mentioned in AI-generated answers.
2. AI-powered content should be structured clearly so AI systems can understand, retrieve, and summarize it accurately.
3. Brands need to build authority, topical depth, and reliable source signals to improve visibility in AI-driven search.
