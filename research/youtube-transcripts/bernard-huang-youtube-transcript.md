## Transcript / Notes

This video discussion explains how AI is changing SaaS discovery, search behavior, and conversion paths. Gaetano DiNardi discusses how users now discover products across Google, LLMs, and video platforms, making traditional SEO traffic and attribution less complete as standalone success metrics.

The discussion highlights the traffic paradox in the AI era, where traditional organic traffic may decline while AI-assisted discovery, brand search, and high-intent conversions become more important. It also explains the dark funnel behavior in B2B SaaS, where users may research through LLMs, YouTube, and other platforms before validating or converting through Google.

For AI-powered SEO content production, this material is useful because it shows that SaaS content should be product-led, experience-driven, and distributed across multiple formats. Written content, YouTube videos, support content, and product knowledge should be repurposed and structured so both humans and AI systems can understand, retrieve, and recommend the brand.

## Key Insights

1. B2B SaaS search is expanding beyond Google into LLMs, YouTube, and other discovery platforms.
2. AI-era SEO should measure visibility, brand discovery, and high-intent conversions, not only organic traffic.
3. SaaS content should be product-led, experience-driven, and repurposed across formats so it can support both human buyers and AI-driven discovery.
