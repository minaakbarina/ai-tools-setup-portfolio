## Transcript / Notes

This video discusses how content marketing is shifting toward zero-click content. Amanda Natividad explains that audiences often consume value directly inside platforms without clicking through to a website. This is increasingly important as social platforms, search engines, and AI systems answer questions or summarize information before users visit the original source.

The video is relevant to AI-powered SEO content production because it shows that content should be useful even when it does not immediately produce a click. For B2B SaaS companies, this means creating content that builds awareness, trust, and recall across search, social platforms, newsletters, communities, and AI-driven discovery channels.

Amanda also discusses practical ways to turn existing long-form content into zero-click content. This is useful for AI-assisted content workflows because one strong source asset can be repurposed into platform-native posts, summaries, short insights, and educational content that reaches buyers where they already spend time.

## Key Insights

1. Zero-click content delivers value directly where the audience already is, without requiring an immediate website visit.
2. AI-powered SEO content should support awareness and trust even when clicks and attribution are harder to track.
3. Long-form content can be repurposed into platform-native zero-click assets that improve reach, brand memory, and buyer education.
