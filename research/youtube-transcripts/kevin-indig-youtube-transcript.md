## Transcript / Notes

This video discusses how AI is reshaping search, visibility, and the role of SEO. Kevin Indig explains that SEO is moving beyond traditional keyword ranking models because users now discover information through Google, ChatGPT, AI Overviews, and other AI-powered search experiences.

The discussion highlights that brands need to think about visibility in a broader way. Instead of focusing only on ranking for individual keywords, companies need to understand how AI systems interpret entities, summarize information, and decide which brands or sources to mention.

For AI-powered SEO content production, this material is useful because it shows that content should help AI systems understand the brand, its expertise, its product category, and its relevance to specific user problems. Content should be structured, credible, specific, and supported by clear topical authority.

## Key Insights

1. SEO is shifting from keyword ranking to broader AI-driven visibility and brand influence.
2. AI-powered SEO content should help search engines and LLMs understand the brand as an entity.
3. B2B SaaS companies need content that builds topical authority, trust, and clear positioning across both traditional search and AI search systems.
